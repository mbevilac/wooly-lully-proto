The Navy Cashmere Crewneck is a wardrobe essential built to last. Knitted from 100% 2-ply Grade A cashmere, it delivers an extraordinary softness that only improves with wear. The classic crewneck silhouette suits everything from smart-casual to relaxed weekend dressing.


The navy colourway is a Swiss wardrobe staple — pairs effortlessly with grey trousers, cream chinos, or dark denim. Finished with clean ribbed cuffs and hem.

Hand wash cold
Do not tumble dry — lay flat to dry away from direct heat
Use a gentle cashmere or wool detergent
Store folded, not hung — hang causes stretching over time
