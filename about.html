Our Mission

Exceptional cashmere and merino, made to last.

Our Materials

What we use and why.

Cashmere — 2-ply, <19 micron
Cashmere is graded by fibre fineness (measured in microns). Below 19 micron is the industry threshold for the finest grade — fibres this fine sit below skin's irritation threshold, meaning no scratchiness even for sensitive skin. Our crewnecks and accessories use this grade exclusively.

2-ply means the yarn is twisted from two strands rather than one. The result: a fabric that holds its shape better over time, pills less in the first few wears, and has a slightly more structured hand feel than single-ply. It's not heavier — it's more durable.

Merino-Cashmere Blend — 70% merino, 30% cashmere
The blend is not a compromise — it's a deliberate choice. Merino wool has natural temperature-regulating properties that pure cashmere doesn't match: it warms when cool and breathes when active. Adding 30% cashmere introduces the softness and drape that makes merino feel exceptional rather than merely functional.

The practical upside: for customers who want cashmere-level softness in a more resilient, thermoregulating sweater they can wear daily, the 70/30 blend is the right answer.
